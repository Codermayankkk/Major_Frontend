How to Use Gmail Genie

Follow these steps to get started with Gmail Genie:

Step 1: Sign Up

Create an account by clicking on the "Get Started" button on the homepage and fill in the required information.

Step 2: Connect Your Gmail Account

Log in using your Google account and grant the necessary permissions for Gmail Genie to access your emails.

Step 3: Configure Settings

Set up your preferences for email filtering and PDF extraction in the settings menu.

Step 4: Start Using Gmail Genie

Once everything is set up, Gmail Genie will automatically filter your emails and extract PDFs based on your preferences.
